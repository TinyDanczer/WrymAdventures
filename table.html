All about the Boxes


There are numerous book boxes, and they vary vastly. Below is a table of boxes I am more familiar with. I have included various qualities, and features found within each one as well. Enjoy the read, and if you have any suggestions for bookish boxes to be added venture over to the survey page and submit a response. I'd gladly add others.

Table Abbreviation Key

  • Q - Quarterly, sub that charges every 3 months.
  • Bi - Bi-monthly, sub that charges every other month.
  • M - Monthly, sub that charges every month.
  • Skips - The number of months you can skip before your sub is terminated.
  • W/O - with out
  • Addtionalt Exclusive Access - Must subs have special edition offered outside of their sub. By being a subscriber of their box you'll typically get early access to these releases.
  • Ya - Young adult themes with age range of 12 - 18. Usually coming of age stories
  • A - Adult books with characters 18+ and usually contain mature or dark themes best suited for an adult audience.
